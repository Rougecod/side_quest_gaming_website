WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Side Quest Gaming Center — where passion meets play.

Nestled in the heart of the city, Side Quest Gaming Center is more than just a place to game — it's a community hub built by gamers, for gamers. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to creating an immersive, next-level experience for every visitor who walks through our neon-lit entrance.

Our state-of-the-art facility features premium PlayStation 5 consoles loaded with the latest titles, from electrifying multiplayer battles to breathtaking single-player adventures. Each gaming station is equipped with high-refresh-rate monitors, premium headsets, and ergonomic seating to ensure maximum comfort during marathon gaming sessions.

But gaming isn't all we offer. Our professional-grade pool tables provide the perfect break from the digital world, offering a classic, competitive experience in a modern setting. Whether you're a seasoned player or picking up a cue for the first time, our tables are maintained to tournament standards.

At Side Quest, we believe in creating memories. From Friday night tournaments to weekend gaming marathons, our event calendar is always buzzing with excitement. Our friendly staff is always on hand to help you find your perfect game, set up a tournament bracket, or simply recommend the best strategy to crush your opponents.
